It’s time to ask nature.

AskNature is an open access library of design lessons from the world’s most prolific and ingenious problem-solver.

Learn more about what you can find on AskNature below and visit AskNature.org to experience all of what AskNature has to offer.

Biological Strategies

Discover stories about how nature works.

We’ve curated more than 1700 strategies used by living things to achieve thousands of different functions. Whatever challenge you’re looking to address, nature can serve as your model, measure, and mentor.

Source: Biomimicry Institute / Copyright © - All rights reserved

Innovations

See how others have applied lessons from nature.

Whether you’re building tools for underwater communication or finding new ways to map the cosmos, nature is rife with practical inspiration for the project at hand. AskNature’s innovation database makes it easy to explore a variety of products and design solutions inspired by the natural world.

Source: Biomimicry Institute / Copyright © - All rights reserved

Collections

Explore connections across content types.

Nature is more than the sum of its parts. Collections package biological strategies, innovations, or educational resources together with essays by leading thinkers, scientists, and artists who reflect on unifying themes in biomimicry.

Source: Robert Kneschke / Shutterstock/ Copyright © - All rights reserved

Education Resources

Inspire students with biomimicry.

The world of tomorrow will be designed by the students of today. AskNature has a wealth of tools to help educators bring amazing natural phenomena and important design lessons into the classroom—for a single day or for a whole school year.

AskNature is a project of the Biomimicry Institute, a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting the practice of looking to nature for inspiration to solve design problems in a regenerative way. Learn more at Biomimicry.org.
